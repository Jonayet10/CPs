Pegasus

The Pegasus Beyblade, known for its incredible attack power and agility, has become a legend in the Beyblade community. Its owner was Gingka Hagane.

Special Moves

  • Starblast Attack
  • Storm Bringer
  • Star Booster Attack
  • Cosmic Tornado

Leone

Leone, with its fierce lion spirit, is a Beyblade that excels in defense. Kyoya Tategami, its proud wielder, challenges opponents with its unwavering will.

Special Moves

  • Lion Gale Force Wall
  • King Lion Reverse Wind Strike
